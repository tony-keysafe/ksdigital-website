Privacy Policy - LifeScope

Effective date: 6 March 2026
Company: KS Digital L.L.C-FZ, UAE

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use LifeScope, including third-party integrations and AI-powered features.

1. Who we are

KS Digital L.L.C-FZ is the data controller for your personal data.

2. What data we collect

A. Data you provide

  • Account details: name, email, login identifiers.
  • Profile info: goals, preferences, notes.
  • Support messages.

B. Integration data (WHOOP, Strava, Fitbit, Google Fit)

Only data you approve during authorisation: activity, sleep, recovery metrics, body measurements.

C. Food photos

Temporarily processed for nutrition analysis. Not retained longer than necessary.

D. Usage and device data

Features used, timestamps, app version, diagnostics.

3. AI-Powered Features and Third-Party AI Services

Important: You will be asked for explicit consent before AI features access your data. AI features will not activate without your permission.

LifeScope uses OpenAI (openai.com) to power meal photo analysis and the AI Health Coach.

What data is sent to OpenAI

  • Meal photo analysis: food photos you submit.
  • AI Health Coach: your messages, and relevant health context (calorie goals, activity summaries, sleep data, nutrition entries).
  • Your nutritional goals and preferences.

How OpenAI uses this data

Data is processed solely to generate your AI response. By default, OpenAI does not use API data to train their models. See openai.com/privacy.

Your consent and control

  • A consent prompt appears before AI features access your data for the first time.
  • You can decline AI features - core app functionality remains fully available.
  • Withdraw consent at any time in Settings > Privacy.

Calorie calculations

Calorie targets use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, recommended by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics. Learn more. Nutritional values follow the Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI).

4. How we use your data

  • Provide and operate the Services.
  • Sync integration data.
  • Process photo features.
  • Power AI features (with consent).
  • Improve reliability and performance.
  • Provide support.
  • Maintain security and comply with law.

We do not sell personal data or use it for advertising.

5. Sharing and disclosure

  • Service providers - hosting, databases, analytics, support.
  • OpenAI - AI features only, with your explicit consent (Section 3).
  • Integration providers - to operate connections you request.
  • Payment processors - if paid features apply.
  • Legal authorities - where required by law.

6. International transfers

Data may be processed outside your country, including by OpenAI in the United States.

7. Data retention

  • Account data: while active.
  • Integration data: while connected.
  • AI conversations: deletable at any time.
  • Support messages: as needed.

8. Security

We use access controls and encryption in transit to protect your data.
